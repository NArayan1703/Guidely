Help center

Frequently asked questions

The essentials before you begin.

What is Guidely?

Guidely is a marketplace for local guided experiences in Nepal. Travellers can browse packages and request dates directly from a guide.

Does a booking request confirm my trip?

No. A request is not a payment or confirmation. Your guide reviews it first, then you can coordinate the next steps.

How are guides verified?

Guide profiles are reviewed by Guidely before approved guides and their active packages are shown to travellers.

Can I become a guide?

Yes. Create a guide account in the Guidely app, complete your profile, and submit it for review.

Privacy policy

Your data, handled carefully.

Guidely collects account, profile, booking, and support information needed to operate the service. We use it to provide bookings, guide verification, support, and account security.

We do not sell personal data. Booking information is only visible to the traveller, their guide, and authorized administrators where needed to run or protect the service.

You can request help with account or data concerns through the in-app Contact us section.

Terms of service

Travel with care.

Guidely connects travellers and local guides; it does not itself operate each guided experience. Guides and travellers must provide accurate information and communicate respectfully.

A booking request is not a confirmed trip or payment. Guides may accept or decline requests. Users must follow local laws and safety guidance during an experience.

We may restrict accounts that misuse the platform, harm others, or violate these terms.
